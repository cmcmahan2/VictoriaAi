# Pacific Web Builder — Sales Playbook v3
### Rental-First · Value Ladder · Recurring Revenue

---

## The Model in One Sentence
Find BC small businesses with no/weak website, hand them a pre-built site for free, charge $99/mo hosting — then walk them up a value ladder to $500–1,500/mo over 6–12 months.

---

## Why Rental-First Beats One-Time Fees

| Old approach | New approach |
|---|---|
| Charge $399 up front | $0 down — "just pay hosting" |
| Client needs to *trust* you first | No trust required — site already exists |
| One payment, then start over | $99/mo × 50 clients = $4,950/mo base |
| Hard close | Easy yes |

A $399 sale is a one-time event. A $99/mo rental is $1,188/year — and it's the door to upselling $300–1,000/mo in services on top.

## Pricing Tiers

### Tier 0 — Free Preview (your bait)
- **What:** Pre-built site deployed to Netlify. No pitch, no commitment, no invoice.
- **Script:** "I already built it. Here's the link. No strings attached."
- **Goal:** Get them on the phone while they're looking at their own site live.

### Tier 1 — Website Rental · $99/mo
- **What:** Hosting + basic maintenance. Site stays live as long as they pay.
- **Setup fee:** $0
- **Netlify cost to you:** $0
- **Profit:** $99/mo pure margin
- **Stripe product:** "Pacific Web Builder – Website Hosting"

### Tier 2 — Growth Plan · $299/mo
Tier 1 plus:
- Google Business Profile setup + monthly optimization
- Responding to reviews every month
- 4 scheduled social posts/mo (Google + Facebook)
- **Stripe product:** "Pacific Web Builder – Growth Plan"

### Tier 3 — Pro Plan · $599/mo
Tier 2 plus:
- Weekly social posts (Google, Facebook, Instagram)
- Monthly SEO blog post
- Review request campaign (SMS/email to their customers)
- Monthly performance report
- **Stripe product:** "Pacific Web Builder – Pro Plan"

### Tier 4 — Premium Plan · $1,200–1,500/mo
Tier 3 plus:
- Google Ads management (they pay ad spend separately)
- Monthly strategy call
- Priority support
- **Stripe product:** "Pacific Web Builder – Premium Plan"

## The Value Ladder: How to Walk a Client Up

```
Month 0  →  Free preview portal link + phone call
Month 1  →  Close Tier 1 ($99/mo rental — easy yes)
Month 2  →  "Nobody can find you on Google Maps yet..." → Tier 2 ($299/mo)
Month 4  →  "Your competitors are posting every week..." → Tier 3 ($599/mo)
Month 8  →  "Want to run Google Ads and double your leads?" → Tier 4 ($1,500/mo)
```

**One client, fully walked up = $1,500/mo = $18,000/year.**
You need 8–10 clients at Tier 3–4 to hit six figures. That is not 500 clients. That is 10 relationships.

## The Upsell Trigger: Your Audit PDF

Every pipeline run generates an AI Opportunity Report PDF. This is your upsell menu.

- **"No Google Business Profile"** → Tier 2 upsell ($299/mo)
- **"Not ranking in local search"** → Tier 2–3 upsell
- **"No reviews / few reviews"** → Tier 2–3 upsell
- **"No social media presence"** → Tier 3 upsell ($599/mo)
- **"Competitors running Google Ads"** → Tier 4 upsell

When a Tier 1 client pays you the second month, send them their audit PDF:

> "Hey [Name], I ran a quick audit on your online presence. Found a few things costing you customers — thought you'd want to see it. PDF attached."

Then schedule a call. Walk them up one tier. Repeat in 60 days.

## Objection Handling

**"I already have a website."**
> "I know — I looked at it. It's not loading correctly on phones, and Google can't read it properly. I built you a new one. Want to see it before you decide? It's free to look."

**"I don't have a budget for this."**
> "Totally fair. There's no setup fee and it's $99/mo — less than your phone bill. If it doesn't bring in a single extra customer in 90 days, I'll refund it. No risk."

**"I need to think about it."**
> "No problem. The site is already live at that link — it's yours. I'll follow up in a week. If you want it pointed to your domain before then, just text me."

**"My nephew makes websites."**
> "That's great. This might actually save him work — the site is already done and hosted. He can focus on other things for you."

## Getting Clients — Week 1 Action Plan

1. **Run province-wide search** in the dashboard. Business type: "restaurant", "plumber", "landscaper", "salon". Province-wide. Max leads: 50.
2. **Filter to score 7–10** (no website or broken/outdated site).
3. **Run pipeline on top 10 leads.** Takes ~5 min each. You now have 10 live preview portals.
4. **Call all 10 today.** Use the phone script above. Aim for 3 demos.
5. **Close at least 1 at Tier 1.** One client = $99/mo. That's your proof of concept.
6. **Repeat next week with a different business type or city.**
